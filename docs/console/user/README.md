## Quick Start

### First Time Playing?

1. **Read**: [Quick Reference](quick-reference.md) - Get familiar with basic controls
2. **Launch**: Start the game client
3. **Practice**: Try basic movement (WASD or arrows)
4. **Explore**: Press **1** for Torch mode and start exploring
5. **Learn**: Check [Gameplay Guide](gameplay.md) for deeper mechanics

### Essential Keys to Remember

- **WASD** or **Arrows** - Move around
- **1** - Torch mode (your go-to lighting)
- **G** - Pick up items
- **O** - Open doors
- **Esc** - Quit

That's enough to start playing! Learn the rest as you go.

## Vision & Lighting System

One of the unique features of Console Game is the dynamic vision system:

| Mode | Press | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Torch** | 1 | Light follows you - classic dungeon exploration |
| **Sunlight** | 2 | Day/night cycle - best for outdoors |
| **Infrared** | 3 | Heat vision - track creatures and see trails |
| **IR + Sun** | 4 | Infrared with daylight - outdoor tracking |

**Pro Tip:** Mode 1 (Torch) is your default. Switch to Mode 3 (Infrared) to track NPCs or find creatures!

## Key Concepts

### Movement
Your character moves **relative to facing direction**:
- **Forward (W)** moves where you're looking
- **Backward (S)** moves opposite direction
- **Left/Right (A/D)** strafes sideways
- Rotate with **Q/E** (fine) or **Z/C** (90° turns)

### Vision
What you can see depends on:
- **Lighting mode** (Torch, Sunlight, etc.)
- **Light sources** (your torch, sunlight, environmental lights)
- **Line of sight** (walls block vision)
- **Directional mode** (toggle with **T** for cone vision)

### Time
The game world has a real-time day/night cycle:
- 60× speed (1 real minute = 1 game hour)
- Visible in Sunlight mode (press **2**)
- Sunrise: 6 AM (red/orange)
- Noon: 12 PM (bright)
- Sunset: 6 PM (red/orange)
- Midnight: 12 AM (dark)

## Learning Path

### Beginner
1. Master basic movement (WASD)
2. Learn to pick up items (G) and open doors (O)
3. Practice rotating to look around (Q/E)
4. Explore with Torch mode (1)

### Intermediate
5. Try different vision modes (1-4)
6. Use Infrared (3) to track NPCs
7. Navigate multi-level dungeons (R/F)
8. Practice efficient exploration patterns

### Advanced
9. Master directional vision (T)
10. Use heat trails strategically
11. Time outdoor activities with day/night cycle
12. Combine vision modes tactically

## Tips for New Players

✅ **DO:**
- Start with Torch mode (1) for general exploration
- Rotate frequently to check your surroundings
- Close doors behind you for safety
- Pick up everything until you learn what's useful
- Experiment with different vision modes

❌ **DON'T:**
- Panic if you get lost (press J to teleport)
- Forget to check your heading (use compass)
- Ignore heat trails in Infrared mode
- Stay in one mode - switch based on situation
- Rush through areas without scanning

## Feature Highlights

### 🔦 Dynamic Lighting
Switch between Torch, Sunlight, and Infrared vision modes in real-time. Each mode offers different strategic advantages.

### 🌡️ Heat Vision
Track creatures and NPCs by their heat signatures. See trails that fade over time, revealing recent movement.

### ☀️ Day/Night Cycle
Experience a full 24-hour day/night cycle in Sunlight mode. Watch sunrise and sunset with atmospheric color effects.

### 👁️ Directional Vision
Toggle between omnidirectional and realistic cone-based vision. Challenge yourself with limited field of view!

### 🎨 Multiple Themes
Cycle through different visual themes (press **H**) to find your preferred color scheme.

### 🔊 Audio Feedback
Immersive sound effects for footsteps, interactions, and environmental cues.
